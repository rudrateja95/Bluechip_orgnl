About Us

About Our Company

  • Bluechip Services International Pvt. Ltd. is an organization specializing in providing comprehensive human resource deployment solutions to multinational and large corporations worldwide. Established in 1999 by a team of young HR professionals with academic expertise and extensive experience in staffing, recruitment, and placement, the company is headquartered in India and has a strong presence in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Malaysia, and the Middle East, with plans to expand into other developed countries.

  • Equipped with the necessary infrastructure and resources to meet industry standards and client-specific requirements, the company's workforce is proficient in matching clients' workforce needs. Qualified HR professionals diligently identify suitable candidates for various job roles. Furthermore, Bluechip International engages high-caliber consultants strategically located to facilitate the recruitment of local talent for international placements.
