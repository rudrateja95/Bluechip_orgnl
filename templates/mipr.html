About

Medical Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research

  • The main goal of the direction of medical care is to train highly qualified doctors who meet the requirements of modern medicine based on the state education standards of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
  • The knowledge provided by leading teachers with strong scientific potential and rich experience working in our institute gives our students the opportunity to compete with students of the most prestigious higher education institutions in the world in terms of knowledge, skills and abilities. Our students study in modernly equipped classrooms and develop practical skills in laboratories, as well as practice in leading private clinics, state hospitals and medical centers, all above mentioned create the ground for them to be the best doctors.
  • It is our constant endeavor to provide the best possible learning environment for our students, which is why we comply with the NMC GUIDELINES CRITERIA FOR STUDENTS FROM INDIA TO STUDY MEDICINE ABROAD to ensure that our Indian students can complete the program without any complications or hassles.
