    Reading Multiple-choice questions, fill in the blanks, re-order paragraphs 32-41 minutes Reading comprehension, text analysis
    Listening Summarize spoken text, multiple-choice questions, fill in the blanks 45-57 minutes Listening comprehension, note-taking skills
