Tsampi Medical Institute

  • Tsampi Medical Institute, Tashkent was organized in 1972 and is the leading institute in the field of education “Pediatrics”. Over the 46-year period of its work, more than 29,500 highly qualified doctors have been trained. TSAMPI is a state higher educational institution that carries out educational activities to implement basic and professional educational programs of higher education, as well as educational, methodological, scientific activities in accordance with the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
  • The structure of the institute includes 39 departments. The teaching staff includes 516 employees, of which 76 are doctors of science and professors, 201 are candidates of science and associate professors.
